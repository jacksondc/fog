Fog

Fog is a text ed­i­tor for pub­lic places. It blurs what you type to make it dif­fi­cult to read over your shoul­der. It works best in either the desktop app or Chrome or Sa­fari, be­cause it uses a se­cret trick1 that is only supported by those brows­ers.
